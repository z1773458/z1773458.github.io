OFFICIAL BLACK KEYS WEBSITE:

The Black Keys are an American rock band formed in Akron, Ohio, in 2001. The band's raw blues rock sound draws heavily from Auerbach's blues influences which include Junior Kimbrough, Howlin' Wolf, and Robert Johnson.

Members of The Black Keys

  • Dan Auerbach
  • Patrick Carney

My Favorite 3 CDS

Brothers
Dan and Partick eventually moved to Nashville in 2010 where my favorite album emerged. The group's sixth studio album, Brothers, was released on May 18, 2010.
The Big Come Up
The band's debut album, The Big Come Up, was recorded entirely in Patrick's basement on an 8-track tape recorded in lo-fi and was released in May 2002. The album features a mix of eight original tracks and five cover songs.
Thickfreakness
Thickfreakness was recorded in Patrick's basement in a single 14-hour session in December 2002.
